Direction & Writing

Lane Haven

Writer / Executive Producer / Co-Director

Lane Haven is an actor, director, and writer from Keller, Texas. He is studying theatre at the University of Southern California with a minor in cinematic arts. He has been doing theatre since 6th grade, and OPHELIA is his attempt to bring the emotional force of live performance into film through a bold, modern lens.
